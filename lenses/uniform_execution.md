## Uniform Execution

Some systems are not broken by incorrect results,
but by differences in how those results are produced.

When execution varies based on hidden values,
those variations can become observable.

---

### Observation

If two inputs produce the same type of result
but follow different paths to get there,
the path itself becomes a signal.

---

### Takeaway

To hide sensitive information,
it is not enough to protect the data —
the process must reveal nothing about it.
